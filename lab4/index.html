What we stand for

Here at RighteousBones, we are proud to be Christian, and we wear it on our sleeves (literally)! It's even in our name, which is inspired by Psalm 34: 17-20. Our image is to be a light and inspiration to the world, and we do so in a fun and fassionable way. Whether you are a person of faith, want to support us, or just want high quality clothes from a brand that cares about its customers, we've got something for you! We also have a podcast and youtube channel which we upload to weekly. And make sure to check out our collection of music that you'll be singing along to in no time! Our design artists work tirelessly to bring you fresh, clean new designs every month. We've set up a gallery to showcase their work, so make sure to show them some support by taking a look around!
